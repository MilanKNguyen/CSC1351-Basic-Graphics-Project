This program is a game.
The goal of the game is to find and capture all of Milan's pets that have escaped.
There are only pets on the Tutorial and LSU maps.

To run the program, enter a username (optional) and then click "start game".
Then, select a character.
Use WASD or arrow keys to move, and space bar to perform an action.
Talk to Milan first (your character and Milan to be touching and you have to press space).
Then follow her directions to begin finding her pets (you will have to enter her home, and then get her pet list from the bookshelf to begin- press p to open the pet list).
